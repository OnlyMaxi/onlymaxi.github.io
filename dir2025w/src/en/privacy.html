Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. General Information

Protecting your personal data is important to us. This website is operated by CuREx GmbH, Vienna, Austria, and is intended to provide information about the CuREx software and related services.

2. Website Usage

This website does not use cookies, tracking technologies, analytics tools, or third-party services.
No personal data is collected automatically when you visit this website.

3. Contact

If you contact us via email or a contact form, the data you provide will be used solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry. No data is shared with third parties and no data is stored longer than necessary.

4. CuREx Software

The CuREx software does not collect, transmit, or analyze personal user data.
File scans are performed locally on the user’s system. No scanned files, scan results, or usage data are transmitted to CuREx GmbH.
Optional updates to malware definitions are initiated by the user and do not involve the transfer of personal data.

5. Donations

Donations offered on this website are voluntary. Payment processing on this website is mocked for demonstration purposes and no real transactions or personal payment data are processed.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or delete personal data related to you.
If you have any questions regarding data protection, you may contact us at any time.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect legal or technical changes. The current version is always available on this website.
